About the Project

The scope of this project is to explore the feasibility of creating and using a game to generate ideation and thought from the game's players about the city they live in and the issues that affect it.

The aim of the game is for players to use it as a platform to develop ideation in order to tackle different challenges faced by Limerick (Transport, Pollution, Derelict Sites etc) that the community can engage in a cooperative and fun way.

Participants will be encouraged to cooperate with each other to develop their own ideas as the end objective is reliant on their decision-making. During the game, participants will have to deal with constraints such as resource management, event cards and the roll of dice that influence city management and development.

The goals for the project boil down to creating a fun and mechanically sound game that encourages discourse and ideation from participants. It also boils down to being as a tool that can help get the city community involved in tackling issues, such as pollution and congestion.

This website in its current state is a prototype to act as a companion to the design game, the main functions of this website are:

  • Provide information about the project
  • Show updates to the city based on what has been learnt from the design game sessions
  • Provide a place for people to download rules and material for the design game so they can run their own sessions
  • A place for people to organise their own sessions and maybe even provide feedback or discuss issues
